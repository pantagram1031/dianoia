## H1 Direct Attack
For every finite division ring D, D is commutative.
Refutation condition: class-equation and maximal-subfield normalizer route cannot force the center to be all of D.
Sanity check: all finite subfields are finite fields; multiplicative groups of subfields are cyclic; the missing step is the normalizer/counting lemma.
Status: survives but high risk.
