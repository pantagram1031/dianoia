## Modification

The benchmark asks whether every finite subgroup `H <= G` has an irreducible
representation of `G` whose restriction to `H` is faithful and
center-preserving.

This modifies the paper by deleting the eligibility condition that `H` has a
faithful irreducible representation and by flattening an iff characterization
into an unconditional universal claim.
