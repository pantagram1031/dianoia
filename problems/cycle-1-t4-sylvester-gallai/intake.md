# Intake

## A. Formal Restatement
For every finite set S of points in R^2 with at least one noncollinear triple, there exist distinct A,B in S such that the line AB contains no point of S other than A and B.

## B. Classification
MSC 2020: 52C10, 51M04. Type: proof.

## C. Success Criteria
Primary target: meta-goal (a), rigorous theorem with proof.

## E. Risk Register
- Overclaim risk: assuming a generic position not present.
- Underclaim risk: proving only for no-three-collinear sets.
- Known-result risk: classical theorem; headline must state classical status.
