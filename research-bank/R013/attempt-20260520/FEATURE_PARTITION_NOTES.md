## Structural Signal

The feature table suggests the following working split:

1. Dense adjacent middle layer (`r1_to_r2 >= 3`) often gives a direct safer
   pair, already seen in the `2/5` buckets and one `13/32` bucket.
2. Dense bottom adjacent layer (`r0_to_r1 >= 3`) gives the dual-looking
   companion cases, including the other `13/32` bucket.
3. The restricted extremal has neither dense adjacent feature but has two
   bottom-to-middle skip covers and one middle-to-top skip cover.

This supports a local-degree proof strategy: prove that dense bottom or dense
middle adjacency forces a pair with lower probability at least `13/32`, then
isolate sparse skip-heavy matrices for exact enumeration.
