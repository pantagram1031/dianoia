# Intake

## A. Formal Restatement
Decide whether the set {n in N : n is perfect} is infinite or finite, by proving one of the two alternatives.

## B. Classification
MSC 2020: 11A25. Type: open problem / proof-or-disproof.

## C. Success Criteria
Primary target: meta-goal (a) for one of the two alternatives. Honest failure is acceptable only after direct attempts and survey evidence.

## E. Risk Register
- Overclaim risk: proving only infinitely many even perfect numbers conditional on infinitely many Mersenne primes.
- Underclaim risk: reporting the known Euclid-Euler classification as if it decides infinitude.
- Open-problem risk: fake SUCCESS-MEANINGFUL would indicate a broken meaningfulness gate.
