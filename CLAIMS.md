# Claims

Every possible novel mathematical claim must be logged here before it can be
treated as a research contribution.

| id | date | claim | type | openness trail | adversarial summary | formal/computational evidence | confidence 1-10 | status |
|----|------|-------|------|----------------|---------------------|-------------------------------|-----------------|--------|

Status values:

- `CANDIDATE`
- `PARTIAL-PROGRESS`
- `OBSTRUCTION-FOUND`
- `SOLVED-CLAIM-PENDING-GATES`
- `DOWNGRADED`
- `BLOCKED-EXTERNAL-REVIEW`

No row may be marked as externally confirmed inside this repo.
