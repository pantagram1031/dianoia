# Intake

## A. Formal Restatement
For every finite division ring D, multiplication in D is commutative; equivalently, D is a field.

## B. Classification
MSC 2020: 16K20, 12E20. Type: proof.

## C. Success Criteria
Primary target: meta-goal (a), rigorous theorem with full proof.

## E. Risk Register
- Overclaim risk: citing the theorem itself as support.
- Underclaim risk: proving only that maximal subfields are commutative.
- Multi-session risk: class-equation proof has a normalizer/counting step that must not be handwaved.
