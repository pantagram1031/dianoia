# Hypotheses Live

## H1 Direct Attack: infinitude
There are infinitely many perfect numbers.
Refutation condition: the attempt relies on unproved infinitude of Mersenne primes.
Sanity check: Euclid-Euler gives even perfect numbers from Mersenne primes but does not prove infinitely many such primes.
Status: killed.

RETREAT TRIGGER: infinitude route requires the open Mersenne-prime infinitude problem.

## H2 Direct Attack: finiteness
There are finitely many perfect numbers.
Refutation condition: the attempt requires excluding infinitely many even perfect numbers and ruling out all but finitely many odd perfect numbers.
Sanity check: known necessary conditions for odd perfect numbers do not imply nonexistence or finiteness.
Status: killed.

RETREAT TRIGGER: finiteness route requires unresolved odd-perfect-number and Mersenne-prime barriers.
