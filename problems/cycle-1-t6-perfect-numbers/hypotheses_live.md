# Hypotheses Live

## H1 Direct Attack: infinitude
There are infinitely many perfect numbers.
Refutation condition: the attempt relies on unproved infinitude of Mersenne primes.
Sanity check: Euclid's construction is conditional on prime 2^p-1 and does not supply infinitely many such p.
Status: killed.

RETREAT TRIGGER: infinitude route requires the open Mersenne-prime infinitude problem.

## H2 Direct Attack: finiteness
There are finitely many perfect numbers.
Refutation condition: the attempt requires excluding all but finitely many odd perfect numbers and all but finitely many Mersenne prime exponents.
Sanity check: current survey gives necessary conditions but no finite bound.
Status: killed.

RETREAT TRIGGER: finiteness route requires unresolved odd-perfect-number and Mersenne-prime barriers.
