# Result

Headline: A classical Gauss-lemma lattice-count proof establishes quadratic reciprocity.

For distinct odd primes p and q, `(p/q)(q/p)=(-1)^(((p-1)/2)((q-1)/2))`. The proof uses the verified Gauss lemma dependency and proves the parity identity by partitioning the rectangle of lattice points by the line `iq=jp`.
