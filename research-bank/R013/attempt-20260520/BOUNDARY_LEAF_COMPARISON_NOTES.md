## Interpretation

The previous hope for a simple branch-by-branch pairing is too optimistic. The
shared `19/13` split survives, but it is distributed through different local
states:

- P7 gets two forced `8/0` majority leaves and one forced `0/9` minority leaf.
- P8 spreads both orientations through three unseen residual leaves.

The promising comparison is therefore not leaf equality. It is a compression
argument: combine P7's forced leaves into aggregate majority/minority blocks,
then compare those blocks against the three P8 unseen residual subproblems.
