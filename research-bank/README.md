# Research Bank

This directory holds candidate open mathematical problems for P10 curation and
P11 attempts.

Each candidate should live under `research-bank/<id>/` with:

- `SOURCE.md`: source problem/conjecture/bound, full citation, and source URLs.
- `OPENNESS.md`: three-source openness verification within seven days.
- `TRACTABILITY.md`: why the problem is small enough to attempt and how dianoia
  might add value.
- `log.md`: attempt log, verdicts, and links to dianoia/raw artifacts.

Candidate row format for future `INDEX.md`:

| id | source | area | openness verified date | tractability rank | why dianoia adds value | status |
|----|--------|------|------------------------|-------------------|------------------------|--------|

P10 target before P11 scale-up: at least 20 verified-open candidates across at
least 4 mathematical areas.
