# Hypotheses Live

## H1 Direct Attack
For every finite division ring D, D is commutative.
Route: use the center F, maximal subfields K/F, cyclicity of K^*, and conjugation/normalizer counts to show no noncentral element can persist.
Refutation condition: the class-equation and maximal-subfield normalizer route cannot force the center to be all of D without importing Wedderburn-equivalent cyclic algebra facts.
Sanity check: all finite subfields are finite fields; multiplicative groups of subfields are cyclic; the previous normalizer/counting lemma is attacked directly.
Status: survives but blocked at a sharper obstruction.

## New obstruction
The normalizer attack reduces to proving that conjugates of a maximal subfield either cover noncentral elements in a count compatible with |D^*| or force a proper normalizer whose quotient action supplies the missing divisibility. The exact no-double-counting argument over the shared center F is not yet closed.
