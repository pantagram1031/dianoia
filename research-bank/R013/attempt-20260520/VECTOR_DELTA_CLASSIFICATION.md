## Interpretation

This is a better finite-subcase skeleton than bucket replay. It says:

1. U6 is the unique vector frontier.
2. Every vector class is obtained from U6 by adding adjacent and/or skip
   placement.
3. The only deltas reaching the processed near-boundary region are the eight
   rows above.

Two visible danger motifs emerge:

- bottom skip plus middle-to-top skip: skip deltas `(1,0,1)` and `(2,0,1)`;
- middle adjacency added without enough top slack: adjacent deltas `(0,1,0)`
  and `(1,0,0)` interacting with bottom skip.

This is still not a proof. It is a finite decision surface that a proof can
try to explain.
