# Panel u03

Question: Is there a plausible direct route to either infinitude or finiteness?

number-theorist: Infinitude would follow from infinitely many Mersenne primes, but that is open and cannot be assumed.

combinatorialist: Finiteness would require bounding odd perfect numbers or Mersenne exponents, neither follows from known divisor constraints.

analyst: Density heuristics do not decide the statement.

Resolution: Both direct routes hit known frontier barriers. Proceed only to document obstruction honestly.
