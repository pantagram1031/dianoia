## Target Statement

Special case of Bonamy-Groenland-Johnston-Morrison-Scott Conjecture 8.4:
let `T` be the directed 3-cycle. Prove that there exists a countably infinite
`T`-free tournament `S_T` such that every nonempty locally finite perturbation
of `S_T` contains an induced copy of `T`.

## Candidate Transformation

Specialize Conjecture 8.4 from all nontransitive finite tournaments to the
directed 3-cycle. This is intentionally narrow: the rational-order tournament
already appears in the source as a single-arc example for Problem 8.3, so the
research value is only in testing whether the locally finite strengthening is
closed, already implicit, or a useful warm-up lemma for larger tournaments.
